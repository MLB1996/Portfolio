About Me

Hi, I’m Michelle — a creative and determined individual from Randburg, South Africa. I've always had a love for art and design, bringing ideas to life using simple tools like PowerPoint, drawing, and now, web development. After caring for a loved one, I realized how much I want to grow, learn, and create freely. While I’ve faced personal challenges like anxiety, depression, and learning differences, they’ve made me more empathetic, resilient, and driven. I’m currently building my skills in web development and design through hands-on projects. Every line of code and design choice is a step toward becoming the person I know I can be — confident, capable, and proud of my journey. If you’re looking for someone who learns through doing, brings creativity to their work, and builds with heart — that’s me.
